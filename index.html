I love learning new skills. I am also happy to meet other people who are exploring and challenging the limits of what they can do. I believe that there is no limit to learning. I am currently working as a Cybersecurity Analyst at a retail corporation. I am enjoying this job and I would like to expand my skills in the software development in order to combine it with my current skills therefore strengthening my aspirations of creating things that would provide protection of human data and privacy. My goal is to continue learning programming as much as I can. I always love to be a beginner. I treat myself as a beginner at all times in order for me to transfer the knowledge and skills to anyone with much more patience and eagerness. Aside from computing, I also have a passion for art.
